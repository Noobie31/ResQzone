What to Do During an Earthquake

  • Drop, Cover, Hold On: Drop to your hands and knees, cover your head, and hold on until the shaking stops.
  • If Indoors: Stay away from windows and heavy objects. Do not use elevators.
  • If Outdoors: Move to an open space, away from buildings, trees, and power lines.
  • If in a Vehicle: Pull over safely and stay inside until the shaking stops.

After the Earthquake

  • Check yourself and others for injuries.
  • Avoid damaged areas and be prepared for aftershocks.
  • Turn off gas, electricity, and water if you suspect leaks or damage.
  • Follow official updates and prepare for evacuation if needed.

Building an Emergency Kit

Make sure to have an emergency kit ready with essentials like:

  • Water (at least 3 days' supply)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First-aid kit
  • Flashlight and extra batteries
  • Personal hygiene items
  • Emergency contact information
